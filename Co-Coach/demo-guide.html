üìñ How to Use

Launch the Application

Open live-posture-feedback.html in Chrome or run start-posture-feedback.bat

Grant Camera Permissions

Click "Allow" when prompted. Camera is required for pose detection.

Position Yourself

Stand 6-8 feet from camera. Ensure full body is visible in frame.

Click "Start Session"

AI coach will activate and begin analyzing your posture in real-time.

Follow the Feedback

Listen to voice coaching and watch the text overlay for specific corrections.

Try the Drills

Click any drill card to activate focused training on specific posture aspects.

How Drills Work

Each drill analyzes specific posture conditions and provides targeted feedback:

  • Perform the drill movement or hold the position
  • Receive real-time feedback on your form
  • Voice guides you with specific corrections
  • Green checkmark = perfect, adjust until you see it!
